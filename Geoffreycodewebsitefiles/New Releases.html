Smile

A chorus opens this epic piano tune and a rousing coda closes it. Through good times and dark times, beginnings and endings, a crack of light through heavy stormclouds, lazy days spent in bed, the song muses upon the power of a Smile to bring happiness, relief, redemption, renewal.
