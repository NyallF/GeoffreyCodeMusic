What is the 'Geoffrey' Code?

Making Music that sounds like it's from yesterday, today. Congratulations, you've found a diamond in the rough! The Geoffrey Code is the jovial, tasteful, and wholesome music you've been looking for this whole time. Please enjoy the wide selection of pop, blues, Christmas Carols and Folk songs available only through the Geoffrey Code.

Personally, I have found that the Geoffrey Code has immensely deepened my appreciation not only for the man behind the music, but for all the beauty we have available to us in life, and for the music that came before that inspired it. A beautiful reminder that no matter how grand our accomplishments, we all stand on the shoulders of Giants. -Nyall Code
